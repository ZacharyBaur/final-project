Markerboard Ledge

2227 Spikes St, Lansing, MI 48906

This rather tall ledge featuring angle iron coping is a popular Lansing skate spot. It has plenty of smooth run-up and run-off, making it ideal for straight grinds and technical tricks. The most difficult part about skating this ledge is the large business sign sticking out of the top of the ledge. Make sure to twist your body to avoid smacking into the sign. Skating during weekdays and business hours is risky, as the area is busy and you may be asked to leave. The spot is best saved for weekends or after-hours when traffic is low.
