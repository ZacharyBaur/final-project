MBC Ledge

435 S Washington Square, Lansing, MI 48933

A crusty and overgrown ledge on the outskirts of a government building's parking lot. The ledge could use some upkeep, and the old wax has mostly sticky and hardedned, giving it a rough surface. As long as the lot is empty, you shouldn't have to worry about being kicked out. The spot is right off the main road, so be mindful of passing cars. The spot is also right next to the bus station, so be mindful of the foot traffic.
