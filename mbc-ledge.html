MBC Ledge

435 S Washington Square, Lansing, MI 48933

A crusty and overgrown ledge on the outskirts of a government building's parking lot. Spot definetely needs some upkeep and the old wax scraped off. As long as the parking lot is empty, you shouldn't have to worry about being kicked out. Watch out for cars and foot traffic from the bus station.
