Broad Art Ledge

547 E Circle Dr, East Lansing, MI 48824

A low angled ledge that works well for wallies and grinds, with two benches on the side that have recently been knobbed. The main ledge is smooth and practical for technical tricks, making it a good spot for practicing lines. Plenty of skaters have hit this spot when visiting Lansing, but it hasn't made n official appearance in any skate videos yet. skaters from the MSU skate club have been known to skate this spot, so keep your eyes peeled for fellow skaters. The spot is right next to Grand River Avenue, so watch out for cars and pedestrians. Getting kicked out is unlikely, but skate respectfully and keep an eye on the surroundings.
