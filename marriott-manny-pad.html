Marriott Manny Pad

300 M. A. C. Ave, East Lansing, MI 48823

Large half-circle manny pad with a long ledge that extends into an out-ledge, backside for regular skaters. The surface is smooth and works well for flat-ground tricks and ledge combinations. Heavy foot traffic and hotel security make it tricky to skate during the day, so this spot is best hit late at night or early in the morning. This is a pretty well known spot out in Lansing, so plenty of tricks have already gone down at this spot. Perfect for testing out manny-grind combos.
